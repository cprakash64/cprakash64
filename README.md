## About

I build AI systems that are meant to work outside the demo environment.

My work focuses on **computer vision**, **private intelligence systems**, **local RAG**, **edge AI**, and **automation** — especially systems that need to operate under real constraints like latency, privacy, reliability, cost, and noisy real-world data.

Currently building **VeoTrex**, a computer-vision platform for industrial safety auditing across construction and logistics environments.
